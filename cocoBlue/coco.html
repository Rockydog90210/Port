coco is a white seal, he was born in las vegas, raised in oregon, during his lifetime, he's seen so many amazing thing, he's gone on many trips. he's been to the beach, to the mountains. he's seen natural rock slide and has even been mountain biking. Coco has been through many adventures, and is excited to see where his adventure takes him next. Maybe he'll use his awesome leadership skills he learned at camp, or maybe he'll show us how to paint. stick around to see what's in store for his next adventure

Through his many adventures Coco has learned so many things. He's a very special seal. He paints and draws and can set up a whole campsite. He can dig awesome holes in the sand which is impressive due to the fact he doesn't have arms. He loves learning many things, and even when they don't go as he planned, he still comes out with knowledge that will help him learn for next time. Coco really is the jack of all trades, though he may not be the master of one, it's better than not having any hobbies. Right now, Coco spends his time learning psychology and biology and even how to code! he loves pushing the limits of what he tries to code, in fact coco helped in the coding of this website! What a versatile little seal. we should all aspire to be more like coco.
